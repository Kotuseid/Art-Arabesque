Instructions:

Lines

  • Hold down the left key on your mouse and drag
  • Move the mouse to the right side of the screen (A selection panel will appear)
  • Select the colour of the line
  • Select the thickness of the line (In pixels)

Centers

  • Right click to create a Center
  • Drawing lines gets rotated and reflected around the active Centers
  • Move the mouse to the right side of the screen (A selection panel will appear)
  • Decide which Centers should be active by selecting the checkboxes
  • Decide how many rotations around the active Centers
  • Note: lines can have multiple Centers

Background

  • Move the mouse to the right side of the screen (A selection panel will appear)
  • Select the color of the background

Keyboard Commands

  • Press Ctrl+z to delete the last line
  • Press Alt+z to delete the last Center
  • Press Ctrl+s to save to image as a png
  • Press the spacebar to clear the screen
  • Note: this removes the lines AND Centers permanently

Press 'i' on your keyboard to open and close this panel

Please open this on a device which has a touchpad/mouse and a keyboard. This does not work on phones or tablets.
